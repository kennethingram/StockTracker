The Covernotes

When I'm not leading technology transformation, I'm on stage with The Covernotes — Ontario's premier live cover band, bringing energy, precision, and unforgettable performances to weddings, corporate events, and private functions across the Greater Toronto Area.

We're multi-instrumentalists and vocalists who pride ourselves on delivering professional, high-calibre performances — from classic rock and soul to the latest floor-fillers. Every set is tailored to the audience and the occasion.
